--- title: What Week Is It @ RIT? subtitle: Answering the age-old, weekly question date: 2015-11-04 tags: - Website ---

Far too often at RIT, I hear "what week is it?" asked between students. Fed up with never knowing, I looked to see if there was somewhere I could find a definitive answer.

<spoiler>There isn't.</spoiler>

Well now there is!

What Week Is It @ RIT? is an silly website that simply answers that question. In our senior year, Shayde Nofziger also added a countdown to graduation.

The site is visible at https://whatweekisit.rit.lol/, and the code is available on GitHub.

Any questions can be directed at RITWeek@corb.co.
