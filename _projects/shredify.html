Shredify.me is a site that matches excellent celebrity photos (like "Shreddy White" here) with excellent versions of related music.

For example, we have:

  • "Taylor Shrift" with a metal cover of "Blank Space,"
  • Barack "Who-Shred" Obama with a homemade metal cover of "Hail to the Chief,"
  • and Reba McEnShred with a metal cover of her '90s hit "Fancy."

The result is available at shredify.me, and the code is available on GitHub.
