Shredify.me (now hosted at shredify.corb.co) is a site that matches excellent celebrity photos (like "Shreddy White" here) with excellent versions of related music.

For example, we have:

  • "Taylor Shrift" with a metal cover of "Blank Space"
  • Barack "Who-Shred" Obama with a homemade metal cover of "Hail to the Chief"
  • Reba McEnShred with a metal cover of her '90s hit "Fancy"

The result is available at shredify.corb.co, and the code is available on GitHub.

Any questions can be directed at shredify.me@corb.co.
