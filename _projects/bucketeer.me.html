Bucketeer.me (now hosted at https://bucket.corb.co) is an online application that lets you record and track things that are on your bucket list. Bucketeer allows you to view the aspirations and achievements of your friends while also congratulating you for completing goals of your own.

Bucketeer was created during BrickHack 2015 at RIT by the team "The Bricks that Hacked."

From the About Bucketeer page

This site was built in a hackathon setting with a team of myself and three others:

  • Val Booth
